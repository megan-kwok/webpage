Cats and Soup

Cats & Soup is a mobile game that allows you to grow a land of cats that make soup! It is the type of game you need when you want to relax and chill. You will be able to earn gold, recipe points and furniture coins when you login everyday! Upgrade your dishes for more benefits.

Expand and explore your land with events!
Dress your cats and decorate their rooms to your liking!
Many adorable resting spots for your hard-working cats!
You can unlock many new tasks for your cats to do!
